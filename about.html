Roop Faridkot

Licensed Life & Living Benefits Advisor

Hi, I'm Roop. I work with self-employed people across British Columbia: the contractors, tradespeople, consultants and small business owners who don't have anybody standing behind them if their health goes sideways.

I know that spot well, because I chose it. I went to school for engineering and spent over ten years in the industry. Good career. But I was sick of working under someone, always answering to a boss, never being able to decide for myself when I could take time off or be with my family. So I started in the financial business part time, built it up, and went full time. That was more than ten years ago, and now this is all I do.

Here's what nobody tells you when you go out on your own. You get the freedom, and you also quietly give up every safety net you used to have without noticing. No sick days. No group coverage. No short term disability. If you can't work on Monday, nobody sends you a cheque on Friday. Most of the people I sit down with have never actually run that math, and it's usually the first time anyone has shown it to them straight.

What I'm genuinely good at is taking complicated financial stuff and making it make sense. Not dumbing it down, just explaining it like a normal person, so you can make an actual decision instead of nodding along. I'm independent, which means I'm not tied to any one insurance company. I shop the whole Canadian market and bring you what's actually best for your situation.

On our call I'll ask about your work, your income, and what you already have in place. Then I'll show you where you stand and what it would cost to cover the gap. If you're already fine, I'll tell you that and let you get back to your day.

Looking forward to talking. No pressure, no obligation, and no jargon. Just a clear picture of where you stand and what your options are.
